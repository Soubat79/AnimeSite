The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten

Romance Comedy Slice of Life Drama

Amane Fujimiya lives alone in an apartment, leading a rather slovenly lifestyle. One rainy day, he finds the school's idol, Mahiru Shiina, sitting alone in a park. She's drenched and looking unwell, so Amane offers her his umbrella and helps her home. This chance encounter marks the beginning of their relationship, as the perfect angel next door starts spoiling Amane rotten with home-cooked meals and tender care.
